The GEOSS Portal and the GEOS DAB evolved thanks to ESA-EC collaboration and has become more user-centric. Different developments are already available from the Operational Portal which is basically focused on discovery and access to data, others are provided as proof of concept in a development portal which is moving towards handling information and services to provide users with the possibility to acquire knowledge and take well-informed decisions.
